## Features
1. **Payment Methods**
   - Credit/Debit Cards
     - Billing Address
     - Zip Code
   - Bank Account Information
     - **USA**
       - Bank Name
       - Type of Account
       - Account Number
       - Routing Number
       - Account Owner Name and Address
     - **Canada**
       - Bank Name
       - Type of Account
       - Account Number
       - Institution
       - Bank Transit Number
       - Account Owner Name and Address
   - Bank Linking by Logging into Your Account
   - Mail Check Payment
     - Fill in Address
     - Fill in Payment Information
     - Amount of Payment
   - Book an Appointment for Payment
